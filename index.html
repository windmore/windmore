When We Invest

Our investment
process

Windmore intervenes at a precise, strategic moment: following the Company Creation phase, after the idea has been selected, the team validated and the concept market-tested.

Phase 01
Idea Selection

The Technical Committee evaluates opportunities and identifies those with the greatest growth potential.

Phase 02
Team Validation

Assessment of the founding team's skills and complementarity relative to the target market.

Phase 03
Market Pre-testing

Concept design and initial tests to validate the business hypothesis before investment is committed.

Phase 04
Seed & Scaling

Windmore deploys seed capital to validate in market and prepare the venture for its growth phase.
